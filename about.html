About Me

Alan Alves

Hi! My name is Alan Alves and I am a massive hip hop fan and enthusiast! My love for hip hop started when I was in middle school. Artists like Travis Scott and many from the "soundcloud rap" era were some of my first true introductions into modern day hip hop and heavily influenced my perspectives on music. Along with that, I have been able to see many new artists appear from the industry, new genres, and countless albums as well as endless hours of music.

My love for hip-hop began as a fan, captivated by the rhythm, poetry, and energy that this genre exudes. One of the artists who truly solidified my passion for hip-hop was Mac Miller. His ability to blend introspective lyricism with infectious beats made his music resonate deeply with me. Mac Miller's evolution as an artist—from his early party anthems to his more mature and soulful works—showed me the depth and versatility that hip-hop artists can achieve. His music became a soundtrack to my experiences, inspiring me to explore the genre even further.

Beyond just listening, I have immersed myself in hip-hop culture by attending concerts and DJ sets. There is something electrifying about being in a crowd, feeling the bass reverberate through the venue, and watching artists bring their music to life. The energy, the passion, and the connection between artists and fans create an unforgettable experience that continues to fuel my love for the genre. Each concert introduces me to new artists and sounds, helping me expand my musical taste and appreciation for different styles within hip-hop.
