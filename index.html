Hip Hop Sub-Genres

Trap Music

Popularized by artists like Gucci Mane and Lil Wayne, trap music uses heavy bass drums and fast hi-hats to distinguish itself from other forms of rap. "Pretty Girls Like Trap Music" by 2 Chainz is a great example of the music and one of the most popular albums of its era.

Cloud Rap

Popularized by artists like Lil B and Yung Lean, cloud rap uses dreamlike beats and layed back tracks to highlight itself and the people that lsiten to it. "Live. Love. A$AP" by A$AP Rocky is a great example of cloud rap and one of the most influential albums of the 2010s
